WHO ARE WE

We Are TDC Media group. We provide complete Brand Marketing, Representation and Technological Consultancy Solutions To help your business achieve the next big goal. Who we are presently is the result of strategic choices, experiences and lessons learned through 15 years of field experiences and working very closely with our clients and solving their problems efficiently. We understand your challanges, your problems and your failures very closely and we have structured our teams to be chanelled around each problem domain you will face. We are the problem solvers for you, with you in your journey

Our Start

2001: We started small as The dealers choice providing Brand Representation Solutions for Banks, Warranty vendors, and suppliers for auto dealerships

The Next Step Stone: Florida

2004: We opened a branch in Florida to cater the needs of our customer and expanded our product and services to banking solutions for small dealers

The Next Move: Mail Service

2007: Feeling the need for advertising and mail marketing for our customers, we added Print advertising and Direct mail services to our arsenal and set up inhouse and outhouse representatives to help our clients 24X7

The Milestone: Web Consultancy

2009: Feeling the Importance of Web Solutions, We set up a development team inhouse to cater the online presense and challanges faced by our clients. We relied on Wordpress and a PhP Backend for our development at the time

E-Commerce and Social Media

2012: Feeling the demand of e-commerce solutions for our clients and reliazing the importance of Social Media Presence, We started providing customized E-commerce and Social Media Packages for our clients

The New Vision

2016: We reformed our company hired smartest professionals in CSR, Analysts, Management and Developers teams. Changed our technology stack, strategies and reframed our approach to provide the best experiences to our clients
